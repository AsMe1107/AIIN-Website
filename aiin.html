Discover more about Asian Indigenous International Network

Asian Indigenous International Network (AIIN) is a regional, non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting the rights, dignity, and development of indigenous communities across Asia. We work to ensure that indigenous peoples are heard, respected, and empowered in every aspect of society.

Our mission is to protect indigenous identities, support cultural preservation, and foster opportunities for education, leadership, and sustainable development. Through collaboration, advocacy, and capacity-building, we strive to strengthen indigenous participation in decision-making and policy processes.

AIIN is built on the foundation of unity, inclusion, and justice. Our network brings together activists, advisors, legal experts, and community leaders to advocate for indigenous peoples' rights and to promote positive change within communities.

We envision a future where indigenous voices are central to building inclusive, equitable, and resilient societies.
