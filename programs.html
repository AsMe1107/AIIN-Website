Our Programs

Initiatives that uplift and empower Indigenous communities across Asia.

Education for Indigenous Youth

Providing access to quality education and scholarship support for indigenous children and youth in remote areas.

Women Empowerment

Training and awareness programs focused on leadership, rights, and health for indigenous women and girls.

Livelihood & Sustainability

Programs that help communities build sustainable sources of income while preserving traditional practices.

Cultural Preservation

Workshops and events dedicated to preserving languages, rituals, crafts, and oral histories of indigenous groups.
