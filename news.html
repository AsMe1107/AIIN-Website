News & Updates

Stay informed with the latest updates from AIIN

Indigenous Leadership Summit 2025 Announced

Published on: May 10, 2025

The Asian Indigenous International Network has officially announced the upcoming Indigenous Leadership Summit 2025. The event will bring together activists, policymakers, and leaders from across Asia to discuss empowerment, equity, and action plans for indigenous communities.

AIIN Launches Educational Scholarship Program

Published on: April 26, 2025

In a move to support underprivileged indigenous students, AIIN has launched a new scholarship initiative. The program will provide financial aid and mentorship to 100 students in its first phase.

New Regional Office Opened in Pokhara

Published on: April 15, 2025

To expand its outreach and presence, AIIN has inaugurated a new regional office in Pokhara. This will serve as a coordination hub for Western Nepal and support community programs in the region.
