Opportunities

We offer various opportunities for indigenous communities and supporters to engage, participate, and grow. Below are some of the key areas where you can get involved:


  • Empowering Indigenous Communities through Opportunities: At AIIN, we believe that equitable opportunities are the cornerstone of empowering indigenous peoples across Asia. Our aim is to uplift marginalized voices by creating meaningful avenues for growth, learning, leadership, and advocacy. We actively engage with community members, youth, professionals, and partner organizations to ensure that everyone has access to opportunities that align with our mission of inclusion and sustainability.

  • Volunteer and Internship Programs: We welcome passionate individuals who want to contribute their time and skills to support indigenous rights and development. Volunteer and internship programs provide firsthand experience working on community projects, research, education campaigns, and organizational development.

Benefits

  • Practical experience in community engagement
  • Skill development in project management, communication, and research
  • Opportunity to work with grassroots leaders and experts

  • Capacity Building and Training: We offer workshops, training sessions, and development programs designed to build the capacity of indigenous youth, women, and leaders. These programs cover topics such as human rights, sustainable development, digital literacy, advocacy, and leadership

Current Focus Areas:

  • Indigenous rights education
  • Climate resilience and environmental protection
  • Gender equality and women’s empowerment

  • Scholarships and Educational Grants: To bridge the education gap, AIIN provides scholarship opportunities for indigenous students pursuing higher education, vocational training, and leadership programs. We aim to support academic excellence and promote lifelong learning.

Eligibility:

  • Students from indigenous backgrounds with academic merit or financial need are encouraged to apply.

  • Partnerships and Collaboration: Organizations and institutions seeking to collaborate on indigenous issues, policy reform, research, or joint advocacy are invited to partner with us. We value synergy and are open to working with both local and international partners who share our vision.

  • Job Openings and Consultancies: As we grow, we regularly announce openings for full-time, part-time, and consultancy positions within AIIN. We seek individuals with a commitment to justice, equity, and indigenous rights.

Watch this page for open calls in areas such as:

  • Project coordination
  • Research and development
  • Community outreach
  • Legal and policy advisory
  • Media and communications

  • Stay Connected: Visit this page regularly or follow us on social media to stay informed about the latest opportunities. Whether you're an individual, student, activist, or an organization, there's a space for you to contribute to meaningful change.
